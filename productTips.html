Product Tips

Please find some care tips for Succulents below.

Succulent varieties require a minimum of 5 hours of sun per day. In extremely hot weather, shade and protection is highly recommended
Our stylistic succulents have been integrated with nurturing features which aid in overall health. As our displays have a multitude of moisture wicking layers, there is no need to worry about drainage. To maintain a healthy succulent, it is recommended to mist daily, and water a few times per month.
